Pong

Level: Beginner
Time: 1 to 3 hours

In this walkthrough you will be using Snap! to create a basic version of the game Pong.  This is a two player game controlled by your keyboard to move the paddles on the screen up and down to block the moving ball from getting passed your paddle and scoring.

Here is what you will do:

  • Layout the graphics on the screen
  • Control the paddles
  • Make a moving ball that bouces off the edges
  • Score when the ball passes a paddle

Don't worry, we walk you through each step.
