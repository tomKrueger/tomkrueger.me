ZTYPE

ZTYPE is a free typing game that is fun for all ages.  
Great sound effects. Fun arcade game play.

I encourcage my kids to play to improve TYPING and READING skills
while getting a break from consuming content.

Easy to Start

Nothing to install

ZTYPE is a space ship shooting game where enemy ships travel to destroy you.  Each enemy has a word associated with them.   Your job is to type the words so your spaceship fires on them and destroys them.  Wooyaaa!

Each level enemies are added or are bigger (having more letters) and they become faster and faster.  So it can get intense.

Word Sets
You can create your own word sets to load.  I have created a few below for my 6 year old who is learning to read.  I think it will be good for her to see the letters grouped together for better recogonition.   And she thinks it is super fun to play.  It's a win win win, learning to type, word recognition, and fun!
